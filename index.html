Biography

The Killers are an American band formed in 2001, in Las Vegas. The Killers are considered to be one of the most successful acts to emerge from Nevada, and are thought to be one of the biggest rock bands in the 21st Century. The Killers have been grouped with many various genres, including alternative, indie, and heartland rock. Band members have also cited influences including artists such as The Smiths, Depesche Mode, U2, and Bruce Springsteen.

Find more information on the band's Official Site.
